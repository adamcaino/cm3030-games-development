## Game Overview 

A 3D, top-down, sci-fi puzzle game where players explore a small laboratory to create a cure for a zombie virus they have been infected with.

Players use point-and-click mechanics to interact with objects, solve puzzles, and gather ingredients for the cure while managing their time before the infection takes over.

## Core Gameplay Loop

- Player explores the laboratory environment.
- Interact with objects to gather ingredients and tools.
- Solve puzzles to unlock new areas and obtain necessary items.
- Manage time effectively to complete the cure before infection progresses.
- Return to the lab bench to combine ingredients and create the cure.

## Key Features

- Point-and-click interaction system for object manipulation.
- Time management mechanic influencing gameplay urgency.
- Puzzle-solving elements integrated into the environment.
- Crafting system for combining ingredients to create the cure.

## Player Experience Goals

- Tension and urgency as the infection progresses.
- Satisfaction from solving puzzles and successfully creating the cure.
- Engagement through exploration of the laboratory environment.
- Immersion in a sci-fi setting with detailed visuals and sound design.
- World-building through environmental storytelling and item descriptions.

## Target Audience & Platform

- Developed using Unity 2019.4 LTS
- PC players who enjoy puzzle and time-management games.
- Targeting casual gamers and fans of sci-fi themes.
- Playable with mouse and keyboard controls.

## Visual Style & Reference

- Visual Style: Clean, futuristic laboratory with a mix of sterile and worn-down elements to convey a sense of urgency and decay.
- Color Palette: Cool blues and whites with contrasting reds and greens for interactive elements.
- Reference Games: "The Room" series for puzzle design, "Papers, Please" for time management mechanics, and "Portal" for environmental storytelling.
